News

Hello everyone. This is Rolland. I am an actor and web developer in Portland, Oregon.

After growing up in Portland and getting my Bachelor of Arts in Theater Arts at Portland State University, I moved to Boston to earn my Master of Fine Arts in Acting at Harvard University while studying at The American Repertory Theater and The Moscow Art Theater. In 2012, I returned to beautiful Oregon and married an even lovelier lady.

Currently, I am a core company member with Third Rail Repertory Theatre and develop websites under the name: Firepoint Creative.
